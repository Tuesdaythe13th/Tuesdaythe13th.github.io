This work represents contributions to NIST's foundational research on trustworthy and responsible AI evaluation. Through collaboration with Humane Intelligence, red-teaming methodologies and safety assessment techniques were incorporated into the ARIA 0.1 pilot evaluation, helping to establish standardized approaches for evaluating AI system trustworthiness at the federal level.

The research informs ongoing development of AI safety policy and provides practical guidance for organizations implementing trustworthy AI systems.
